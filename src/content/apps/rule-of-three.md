## About the App

A simple but effective todo app. Set your goals and focus. Focus on a day, week, month, quarter, or year level.

The methodology is simple. You set three goals for a specific time period. You focus on achieving those goals. Once you achieve them, you set three new goals for the next time period.

## Why Rule of Three?

There are many todo apps out there, but they are often too complex. With the help of **Rule of Three** you can focus on what matters most. Keep your goals easy and achivable.


Continuous progress leads to big achievements. With the help of our app you will work continuously on your goals and soon you will see the results.
